About:

  • To move a full stack, make sure the NUMBER field is set to 0.

  • RESET NUMBER will restore the value to 0.

  • The NUMBER field will NOT reset on close, so get used to CLEARING it!

  • You can DOUBLE CLICK an item to USE.

Shops:

  • Put in the amount you want to buy in the NUMBER field and then drag the shop item into your inventory.

Item Durability:

  • Some items will decay on USE, eventually fading to a reddish color. (Mostly WEAPONS)

  • Once an item reaches 5%, it will become ALMOST destroyed, whilst still being usable.

  • Once an item reaches 0%, it will be DESTROYED and REMOVED from your inventory.

Weapons:

  • CLICK on a WEAPON to VIEW its Attachments.

  • You will need a WEAPON in your inventory to VIEW.
